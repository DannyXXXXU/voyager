## Topic

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park in China's Hunan Province, home to the iconic Avatar Mountains officially known as the Hallelujah Mountains, served as the real-world inspiration for the highest-grossing film of all time. This comprehensive travel guide covers all five distinct park sections, blending record-breaking engineering feats like the Bailong Elevator with natural wonders including 350-meter-high natural archways and towering quartz sandstone pillars that draw over 20,000 visitors daily.
