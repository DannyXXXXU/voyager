## Topic

2 days in Jiuzhaigou lakes & waterfalls solo (Cherie Diaries)

## Top Hooks

- In this chapter of My China Diaries, come explore the beautiful Jiuzhaigou National Park with me.
- It used to take about 8-10 hours from Chengdu by bus but with the opening of the railway station the whole journey has been cut down to around 4 hours in total.
- Jiuzhaigou has been recognised as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO since 1992.
- Pearl Beach was featured in the 1986 version of Xi You Ji which is also known as Journey to the West
- In many ways I do think Jiuzhaigou brings together the worst and the best parts of travelling in China.

## Selling Points

- **UNESCO World Heritage Site with international recognition since 1992** - establishes credibility and prestige for skeptical Western audiences
- **Journey time dramatically reduced from 8-10 hours to just 4 hours total with new high-speed railway** - directly addresses accessibility concerns
- **Famous for spectacular waterfalls and colorful lakes, especially stunning during autumn** - primary visual draw for outdoor and nature content creators
- **Convenient online ticket purchasing for foreign travelers through trip.com using passport** - removes friction barrier for independent travelers
- **Entire scenic area connected by 70km of well-maintained paved boardwalks** - signals accessibility and ease of navigation
- **Affordable refreshments inside the park with grilled sausages at 10 RMB (around 2-3 AUD)** - counters expensive tourism perceptions
- **Featured filming location from the iconic 1986 Journey to the West TV series** - cultural anchor point for Asia-literate audiences
- **Showcase of China's exceptional natural beauty beyond major cities** - positions content in the "hidden gems" narrative

## Themes

**Natural Beauty & Scenic Landscapes** - Jiuzhaigou showcases China's extraordinary natural landscapes with colorful lakes, waterfalls, and high-altitude scenery across multiple valleys.

**Travel Access & Modern Infrastructure** - Modern transportation and world-class infrastructure make Jiuzhaigou highly accessible and well-maintained for international visitors.

**UNESCO Heritage & Cultural Recognition** - Jiuzhaigou holds prestigious UNESCO World Heritage status and served as a filming location for China's legendary Journey to the West.

**Visitor Experience & Local Culture** - The park offers affordable amenities, rich local folklore, and an authentic experience that captures the essence of traveling in China.

## Recommendations

1. Lead with the infrastructure upgrade story (8-10 hours to 4 hours) to immediately address the "China is hard to travel" objection and position the destination as newly accessible.

2. Emphasize the UNESCO designation and Journey to the West connection early in content to establish cultural legitimacy and tap into existing audience knowledge of Chinese culture.

3. Feature practical details like passport-based online booking and affordable food pricing to build trust with independent travelers and counter perceptions of China travel being complicated or expensive.

4. Showcase the 70km boardwalk infrastructure visually to demonstrate that visiting doesn't require extreme fitness or backcountry skills, making the content appealing to broader demographics.

5. Frame the "worst and best parts of travelling in China" hook as an honest, balanced perspective that builds creator authenticity rather than promotional tourism content.
