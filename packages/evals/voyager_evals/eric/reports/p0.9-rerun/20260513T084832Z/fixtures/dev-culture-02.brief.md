## Topic

Laid-back Chengdu teahouse culture showcasing He Ming Tea House, a century-old institution in People's Park that embodies the city's distinctive tea heritage and relaxed lifestyle.

## Top Hooks

- "the most famous tea house in Chengdu is here"
- "It was built 100 years ago"
- "That is so hard. The balance is so critical."
- "snowflakes floating in a green lake"
- "within the top ten teas in China"

## Selling Points

- **Century-old heritage**: Visit He Ming Tea House, established in 1923, the oldest teahouse in Chengdu with nearly 100 years of history
- **Kung fu tea performance**: Watch live demonstrations featuring traditional long-bead teapot with impressive balance and skill
- **Traditional etiquette**: Learn authentic tea-drinking techniques with distinct methods for men and women
- **Premium Chinese tea**: Taste Meng Ding yellow tea, one of the top ten most famous teas in China
- **Historic service signals**: Discover traditional lid-positioning techniques to request water or assistance
- **Laid-back lifestyle**: Experience authentic Chengdu culture starting as early as 7 AM in the morning
- **Central location**: Easily accessible spot right in People's Park at the city center
- **Tea culture capital**: Chengdu has more teahouses than other cities in China, making it the definitive destination for tea enthusiasts

## Themes

**Historic Tea House Heritage**: Visit the oldest and most famous tea house in Chengdu, He Ming Tea House, with nearly a century of history since 1923.

**Traditional Tea Performance Art**: Watch skilled kung fu tea performances featuring the long-bead teapot with impressive balance and traditional demonstration techniques.

**Premium Chinese Tea Selection**: Enjoy top-tier Chinese teas including Meng Ding yellow tea and jasmine tea with poetic names like 'snowflakes floating in a green lake'.

**Tea Culture Customs & Etiquette**: Learn traditional tea-drinking etiquette for men and women, plus historic lid-positioning signals to communicate with staff.

**Chengdu Tea Culture & Atmosphere**: Experience Chengdu's unmatched tea culture capital with vibrant atmosphere at People's Park, where locals gather from 7 AM onwards.

## Recommendations

1. Open with the kung fu tea performance to grab attention immediately, emphasizing the difficulty and skill required for balance
2. Position He Ming Tea House as a must-visit heritage site by leading with its 100-year history and status as Chengdu's oldest and most famous teahouse
3. Highlight the unexpected diversity of visitors, especially the surprising number of young people, to counter stereotypes about traditional tea culture
4. Feature the poetic tea names and premium selections to appeal to both cultural enthusiasts and luxury travelers seeking authentic experiences
5. Emphasize the early morning start times and central location to provide practical travel planning information for international visitors
